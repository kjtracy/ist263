Babylon Docks

Babylon Docks is a place where you can fish and run. I run 2 miles and 3 miles at the Babylon Docks. Babylon Docks is a nice place to chill and fish. You can also see robert moses bridge from here.

Babylon Village Pool

Babylon village pool's address is 700 Fire Island Ave S. Babylon Village Pool has a big parking lot for people to park their cars and run. They also do swimming lessons in the summer from late June-Early September. Babylon Village pool is only open during the summer.

Babylon Village

Babylon Village is a village in long island with lots of restaurants and stores. Babylon Village has a Post Office 5K every october. Every November and May we have parades for Memorial Day and Veteran's Day. Babylon Village is one of the most popular villages in Long Island.

Argyle Lake

Argyle Lake is a park in Babylon, NY. For one lap of Argyle Lake, it is 0.75 miles. Argyle Lake is such a beautiful lake. Argyle Lake is where most of the duckies live.
